Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-08

What we collect

  • Account details you provide (name, email).
  • Follow-up records you create (client name/email, notes, schedule info).

Google/Gmail access

If you connect Gmail, the app requests the minimum permissions needed to send emails on your behalf. The app does not read your Gmail inbox unless you explicitly enable features that require it.

How we use data

  • To run the app features (scheduling, sending follow-ups, account management).
  • To support troubleshooting and improve reliability.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data when required to provide core services (e.g., email delivery) or when legally required.

Security

We use reasonable security practices to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

Contact

For privacy questions, email: support@gmail.com

Tip: If Google asks for justification, say the app uses gmail.send to send scheduled follow-up emails.
